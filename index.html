introduction

A conference by developers,
for developers


BuzzConf is a conference created by developers for developers that explores the new horizons in computer science such as functional programming, distributed systems, big data, machine learning and other interdisciplinary areas and brings them to a bigger audience.

Buzzconf consists in a day of talks given by experts in each area, followed by day of workshops for more in-depth learning.

We strive to create interesting discussions within the community about the challenges and opportunities in the near future of programming.
